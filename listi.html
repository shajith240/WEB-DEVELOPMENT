Shajith's Recipe for the Best cinnamon Rolls

Ingrediants

For the dough :

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 table supoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instrutions

  1. Mix th milk with the yeast,sugar,eggs.
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture
  3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough.
  4. Knead th dough for 10 minutes
  5. transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap.Leave it somewhere to rise for 2 hours.
  6. After the dough get doubled the bake it in the oven with the filling
